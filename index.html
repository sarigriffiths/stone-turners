Who we are

We believe in curiosity as a positive force and an antidote to unthinking consumption. For us, curiosity is essential for a life well lived, rich in discovery and potential. It is our aim to support all the StoneTurners of this world and their natural desire to prod and to poke, to explore and to reveal.

What we do

We provide informative and inspirational content and instructions for how to make and access things that aid discovery. We are inclusive, accessible and open source and aim to engender this approach in others.
